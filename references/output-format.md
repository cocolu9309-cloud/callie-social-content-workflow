# Output Format

Use this template for a full Callie social content pack.

```markdown
## Content Pack: [Campaign or Topic]

### 1. Big Idea
[One clear creative concept.]

### 2. Brand Angle
[How this supports Callie as a personalized gift brand.]

### 3. Product Tie-In
[Category or product connection. Note whether it is light, medium, or strong.]

### 4. Trend Hook
- Trend/pattern:
- Source/check needed:
- Market fit:
- Why it fits Callie:
- Risk level:

### 5. Platform Versions

#### TikTok
- 3-second hook:
- 15-30 second script:
- On-screen text:
- Sound/trend suggestion:
- Caption:
- Hashtags:

#### Instagram
- Reel hook:
- Caption:
- Carousel/Story idea:
- Hashtags:

#### Pinterest
- Pin title:
- Pin description:
- Keywords:
- Board suggestion:
- Vertical visual direction:

#### YouTube Shorts
- Title:
- Script:
- On-screen text:
- Description:
- Thumbnail text:

#### Facebook
- Post copy:
- Conversation prompt:
- CTA:

#### X
- Short post:
- Thread opener or poll:
- Hashtags:

#### Website
- Banner headline:
- Supporting line:
- CTA:
- Optional module copy:

### 6. Visual Direction
[Shot list, image style, text overlay, layout, or pin composition.]

### 7. Keyframe Descriptions
[Table describing the key visual moments across the video or content sequence.]

| Frame | Time | Visual Description | On-Screen Text Suggestion |
|-------|------|--------------------|----------------------------|
| 1     | 0:00 | [Opening shot or hook frame] | [Text overlay at this moment] |
| 2     | 0:03 | [Early engagement frame] | [Text overlay] |
| 3     | 0:08 | [Core content frame] | [Text overlay] |
| 4     | 0:15 | [Peak moment or pivot frame] | [Text overlay] |
| 5     | 0:22 | [Closing frame or CTA frame] | [Text overlay] |

### 8. CTA
[Primary action.]

### 9. Brand Safety Check
[Claims avoided, trend risk, tone notes, market limitations.]
```
